About Nuwara Eliya

Discover the heart of "Little England"

Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England", is a stunning hill station in Sri Lanka. With its cool climate, rolling tea plantations, waterfalls, and colonial charm, it's a paradise for nature lovers and travelers alike.

📜 A Little History

Founded by British colonists in the 19th century, Nuwara Eliya was designed as a retreat from the heat. English-style cottages, a racecourse, and a golf course still stand today. After independence, it became Sri Lanka's most beloved hill country destination.

🌤️ Best Time to Visit

  • February to April – Dry & sunny
  • July to August – Cool & misty
  • December to January – Coldest

🚗 How to Reach

  • From Colombo: 4-5 hours by car
  • Train to Nanu Oya (scenic)
  • Tuk-tuks & taxis locally

🍽️ Must-Try Food
